University Projects

This is a repository of the projects I worked on during my university career. Most of these are more associated to microcontroller programming than software programming, as my career was in Mechatronic Engineering.

Smart stepper motor controller system

This is a system that makes use of several microcontrollers in serial connection where a master microcontroller sends commands and the desired microcontroller within the chain executes said command. For this, a Serial UART communication protocol was used.

Digital clock using PIC16F887

A digital clock was designed using a PIC16F887 microcontroller and Assembly language in the MPLAB development environment. The clock has 3 modes: a digital clock, a calendar (with day and month) and an alarm.
